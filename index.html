SKIMMER POOL

The freeboard swimming pool also widely known as th he skimmer pool has been one of the most frequently built swimming pools.

OVERFLOW POOL

This would be a pool where the water level in the pool is the same as the top of the decking. It looks like a big mirror lying on hte ground.

INFINITY POOL

This is a swimming or reflecting pool which produces a visual effect of water extending to the horizon, vanishing or extending to "infinity".
